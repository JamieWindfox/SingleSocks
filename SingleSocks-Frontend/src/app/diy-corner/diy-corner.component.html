Darn Holey Socks

  1. Prepare what you need: A holey sock, a needle, a thread in the color of the sock, a darning egg or a similar object.
  2. Thread the needle with one or two strands of thread, depending on the weigth of the sock.
  3. Pull the sock over the darning egg and trim away any ragged edges from the sock fabric.
  4. Support the fabric around the hole with basic sewing stitches.
  5. Run the thread up and down the hole until the hole is blocked with parallel stitches.
  6. Sew stitches perpendicular to the parallel stitches.
  7. Be happy with your unholey sock.

Sock Puppets

  1. Prepare what you need: A sock, a needle, a thread, 2 buttons or googley eyes and optionally some felt and/or wool rests.
  2. Try the sock on and see how it fits to become a puppet.
  3. Attach the eyes to the place where it feels right, either by glueing or sewing them on.
  4. Glue and/or saw any remaining attachments on. Be creative! You can make all kinds of sock puppets!
  5. Be happy with your flock of puppets.

Sport Mobile Case

  1. Prepare what you need: A large sock, a smaller sock, a string, a needle, a thread, your mobile phone for measurements.
  2. Cut away the toes of the large sock and put it on your upper or lower arm to see where it fits well.
  3. Put your mobile phone into the smaller sock. It should fit in completely and leave a rim of about 3-5cm.
  4. Saw the string into the rim of the smaller sock to be able to close it, that your mobile doesn't fall out.
  5. Saw the small sock to the big one in the same direction.
  6. Happy running/biking with your phone attached to you without a pocket!
